Exquisite Bio

A love for everything exquisite in fine dining, wine, and music, Spencer Diamond, born in Somerset, Kentucky and raised in Corbin, home of the original Colonel Sanders and Kentucky Fried Chicken, launched his music career a few short years ago. Ready to embark on a new journey, his passion for TrapSoul and R&B music embodies a brand new sound that will tell stories for decades to come. Through his upbringing, he listened to heartthrobs of the 90’s like Backstreet Boys, K-Ci & JoJo, and Bryan McKnight, which inspired his upbeat sound that will draw you in and have you dancing in the kitchen.
Spencer began his career in the restaurant business. His love for fancy food and wine, a self-taught and certified level one Sommelier, has funded his music career while waiting tables and spends the rest of his days writing and recording music when he’s not working.
Combined with rare levels of enthusiasm and his eagerness for success, Spencer’s sound, much like The Weeknd, will take you on a rollercoaster and have you craving the special someone that got away.
He creates a thirst and a passion for all the finer things in life, much like his new and upcoming single, “Merlot.” The song captures the story of options, and acknowledging that while they have plenty, you will always be the best one. While you may have options to listen to other artists, instead, let Spencer’s voice take you where you haven’t been before. So turn up the music, pour a glass of wine because once you start, you won’t be able to stop. At the forefront of American culture, his inspirations for falsettos and reverb-heavy vocals, will become a crossover sensation and a new household name.
